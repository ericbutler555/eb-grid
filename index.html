Basic Grid Examples

class="col half tab-1-3"
class="col half tab-2-3"

The layer above has 2 columns. On a phone (up to 767px wide), the columns will each be 50% of the viewport width. On a tablet (768px) or wider, the first column will be 1/3, and the other will be 2/3 of the viewport width.

If you're on a screen that supports it, resize your browser window to see the sizes change.

class="col tab-half lap-1-3 desk-1-5 pad-all"

This layer has 5 columns. On a phone (up to 767px), the columns will all be full-width and will stack on top of each other. On a tablet (768px-991px), they'll be 2-across, or 50% wide. On a laptop (992px-1199px), they'll be 3-across, or 33% wide. And on a desktop (1200px or wider), all 5 will be side-by-side.

These columns also have the .pad-all class added to them, so they have 10px of padding around all of their sides.
